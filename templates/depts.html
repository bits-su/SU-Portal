Arts and Deco

Someone once said: “An artist can never fail, it is a success just to be one.” (Charles Cooley). So, the Department of Arts and Deco of BITS, unites art, craft and creativity for something that makes a large part of each of the college fests, be it ATMOS, ARENA or PEARL. The light of the department’s works of creativity are displayed in everything. Since it's inception it has been growing every year to new heights. With students from different skill sets and ideas, this department is the largest one of its kind. It's more than just a department, it feels like a family united by hues of every colour.

Controlz

In this department, easy jobs are not done; rather, jobs are made to seem EASY through MANAGEMENT and CO ORDINATION. Starting from the subtle job of escorting chief guests to teeny-weeny jobs like HELP DESKS are ensured by CONTROLZ. Work starts from preparation of schedule for the fest which includes Competitive Events, Informal events, Professional Shows, Workshops and Ceremonies. The most integral part of any fest is accommodation and hospitality of the guests who attend our fests. This is the department which is mainly responsible and ensures that all the deserved are awarded by trophies, medals and certificates. Most awaiting part for all the participants i.e Prize Money /Gift Vouchers and participation certificates too are strictly looked upon to reach the respected ones. The department puts up ANNOUNCEMENT DESKS to make people aware of all the postponements / delays and clarify all their queries. The department works day and night, determined and dedicated to arrange, manage, co-ordinate with the working team of Professional Shows. The department takes pride in wearing our Fest T-Shirts and it is the CONTROLZ Dept which puts up stalls and makes sure that everyone enjoys the fest and has a great time.

DOTA

The Department of Technical Arts (DoTA) is a student body that is responsible for all the technical support in organizing the cultural, technical and sporting festivals at BITS-Pilani, Hyderabad campus. This includes the development of Websites, Mobile Applications, Payment Gateways and Registration Softwares along with graphic content for online publicity and various events happening at the respective festivals.

DePP

The Department of Publicity and Public Relations (DePP) handles the brand marketing of all three campus festivals i.e. ATMOS, the technical festival, PEARL, the cultural festival and ARENA, the sports festival of BITS - Pilani, Hyderabad Campus. To ensure a great participation in these fests and promote it to Students all over the country is the main function of the Department.
The department is currently a team of 75 undergraduate students that overlooks the PR and Publicity portfolios of all college festivals in BITS Hyderabad. There are two teams-Online Publicity Team and offline publicity team. The Department’s work includes:
1. Handling the Social Media coverage of ATMOS, PEARL AND ARENA. The department usually generates a 60% Y-O-Y increase in “Brand” visibility through likes and average post reach.
2. Handling offline and on ground publicity by various campaigns such as PR calls, Campus visits, Campus Ambassador Programme.
3. The department generates revenue through event sales participation.

DoSM

The Department of Sponsorship and Marketing connects us to the corporate world. It associates with companies to build sponsors and generate revenue for our fests while giving the students a rich exposure to interact executives of big corporations and multinationals and helping them develop their personality in terms of soft skills, confidence, negotiations and pitching skills. The sponsor's brand is promoted on various online and offline platforms and provides them marketing support through various avenues.

DoSH

Registrations, accommodations and security are three broad tasks of this department. DoVE

The Department of Visual Effects is a group of enthusiastic and energetic people working all year round to capture glimpses of your unforgettable moments. The Department puts out trailers before the fests, alluring the crowd to come and join us in the extravaganza. During the fests, the Department goes around and records events and pro-shows. To make sure that the joy never fades in your memory, the Department sort the videos into an aftermovie. Anywhere you need a video to be recorded, we are the ones to call. The Department comprises of videographers, editors, film-makers and anything and everything you can relate to ‘Visual Effects’.

Firewallz

Firewallz – The backbone of ARENA. The department of Firewallz’s responsibility begins and ends with only one name – ARENA. Despite being a department only for ARENA, its absence takes away a significant chunk of the success the fest entails. From the smallest jobs like setting up tents and issuing ID cards to the biggest like escorting of guests and security related affairs for any events and pro shows, coordinating between teams, captains and sports secretaries for and providing the hospitality to participants, scheduling games, issuing prize money and certificates, everything that is done, is done by this department alone. Firewallz’s responsibilities other than the above include organizing the inauguration ceremony of ARENA, tracking of losses and damage accurately by handling inventory with utmost caution, scheduling games and matches, verification of details, setting rules and regulations for everyone to follow, etc. The smooth sailing fest happens only because of the ardent cooperation of the department of firewallz and hence it got its title as the backbone of ARENA.
