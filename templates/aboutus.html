Election Commission

The Election Commission (EC) is a six-member, independent and self-sustaining body of the Students' Union (SU). It is be responsible for conducting free and fair elections for all the organs of the SU. The EC also moderates the General Body Meetings (GBMs). Additionally, the EC makes sure that SU Constitution is upheld by all the position holders.

Corroboration and review committee

The Corroboration and Review Committee is a 6-membered neutral body. The CRC audits all receipts and expenditures of the Student Union Council (including the 4 major annuals fests). The CRC is also responsible for scrutinizing and approving the budgets of the above activities and for maintaining and enforcing the Procedure Manual of BPHC.

Students mess council

SMC’s are responsible for addressing concerns regarding all mess affairs. They serve as a bridge between mess caterers and the Union.SMC consists of 5 members elected by the Union.SMC also has a feedback page, where every query, suggestion and criticism is appreciated and properly evaluated leading to continual improvement. SMC makes sure that the terms in the contract are being met. SMC also gives suggestions to the Administration about the vendors in the campus.
